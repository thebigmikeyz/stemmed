UCAT

What it is: The UCAT (University Clinical Aptitude Test) is a computer-based admissions test required by most UK medical and dental schools. It assesses cognitive abilities, attitudes, and professional behaviours.

Who it's for: Students applying to medical or dental school in the UK (and some universities in Australia and New Zealand).

How StemMed Supports You

  • 1-to-1 Tutoring: Personalised sessions focused on all five subtests (Verbal Reasoning, Decision Making, Quantitative Reasoning, Abstract Reasoning, and Situational Judgment).
  • Strategy Development: Customised approaches for each section to maximise your score.
  • Timed Practice: Realistic exam conditions to build confidence and speed.
  • Performance Analysis: Detailed feedback on strengths and areas for improvement.

LNAT

What it is: The LNAT (Law National Aptitude Test) assesses aptitude for studying law at university. It consists of multiple-choice questions based on reading passages and an essay section.

Who it's for: Students applying to study Law at UK universities including Oxford, Cambridge, UCL, King's College London, and others.

How StemMed Supports You

  • 1-to-1 Tutoring: Intensive preparation for both the multiple-choice and essay sections.
  • Critical Analysis Skills: Develop the ability to read, analyse, and critique complex texts quickly.
  • Essay Writing: Structured approach to constructing compelling legal arguments under time pressure.
  • Mock Examinations: Full-length practice tests with detailed feedback.

TMUA

What it is: The TMUA (Test of Mathematics for University Admission) assesses mathematical thinking and reasoning skills required for mathematics-based degree courses.

Who it's for: Students applying to study Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics, or related courses at Cambridge, LSE, Warwick, and other universities.

How StemMed Supports You

  • 1-to-1 Tutoring: Targeted support for both Paper 1 (Mathematical Knowledge) and Paper 2 (Mathematical Reasoning).
  • Problem-Solving Techniques: Develop advanced problem-solving strategies beyond A-Level content.
  • Proof and Logic: Master mathematical reasoning and proof techniques.
  • Past Paper Practice: Extensive practice with official TMUA questions and similar problems.

MAT

What it is: The MAT (Mathematics Admissions Test) is used by Oxford University to assess mathematical ability and potential. It focuses on mathematical reasoning and problem-solving rather than memorised techniques.

Who it's for: Students applying to study Mathematics, Computer Science, or related courses at Oxford University.

How StemMed Supports You

  • 1-to-1 Tutoring: Personalised coaching focused on mathematical thinking and problem-solving approaches.
  • Proof Writing: Develop the ability to construct rigorous mathematical proofs.
  • Oxbridge-Style Questions: Practice with authentic MAT-style problems and past papers.
  • Strategic Thinking: Learn how to approach unfamiliar problems systematically.

ESAT

What it is: The ESAT (Engineering and Science Admissions Test) is Cambridge's admissions test for Engineering, Natural Sciences, Veterinary Medicine, and Chemical Engineering.

Who it's for: Students applying to study Engineering, Natural Sciences, Veterinary Medicine, or Chemical Engineering at the University of Cambridge.

How StemMed Supports You

  • 1-to-1 Tutoring: Comprehensive preparation for all ESAT sections (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology).
  • Subject-Specific Focus: Deep dive into the scientific concepts and problem-solving techniques required.
  • Multi-Disciplinary Approach: Develop the ability to integrate knowledge across scientific disciplines.
  • Cambridge Preparation: Familiarisation with Cambridge's assessment style and expectations.
